Privacy Policy for Twit

Last updated: October 26, 2025

Twit (“we,” “our,” or “us”) operates the Twit mobile application (the “App”). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you sign up or log in, we collect your name, email address, and profile image.
  • User Content: Any posts, comments, likes, messages, or other content you create or share within the app.
  • Device Information: Basic technical information such as device type, operating system, and app version to help improve performance.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics to understand how users interact with the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, operate, and improve the Twit app experience.
  • Allow users to interact through posts, likes, comments, and chat.
  • Personalize your experience and display relevant content.
  • Communicate with you regarding app updates or issues.
  • Maintain safety and prevent spam or misuse.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share limited data only when necessary:

  • With service providers (e.g., hosting or analytics) who help us operate the app.
  • When required by law or to protect the rights and safety of users or others.

4. User-Generated Content

Anything you post publicly (tweets, images, comments, etc.) may be visible to other users. Please avoid sharing personal or sensitive information in public posts.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as your account is active or needed to provide the service. You may request data deletion by contacting us.

6. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no system is completely secure.

7. Children’s Privacy

Twit is not directed to children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us for removal.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted here with a new “Last updated” date.
